Pink Ladies

Every Wednesday in Room 125


Pink Ladies is a group that will be meeting monthly and is open to all students who identify as female. The meetings are centered around specific topics that impact young women in our community. The group is designed to provide a safe space for students to share ideas, issues and have an open dialogue.



Topics Discussed

  • Beyond Girl Code
  • Women in Culture
  • Women viewed in Religions
  • Stereotypes of Women
